Disclaimer & Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

Educational purposes only

The Clinical Perspective publishes summaries and commentary on published research for educational and informational purposes. Nothing on this site or the associated Instagram account is medical, psychological, or professional advice, and nothing here creates a clinician–patient relationship. Do not use this content to diagnose or treat any condition, or to start, stop, or change any treatment. Always consult a qualified health professional about your specific situation.

If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself, contact your local emergency services. In the United States, you can call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) at any time.

About the summaries

Summaries are prepared with care, but they are secondary descriptions of other people's research. The original published paper is always the authoritative source, and every summary links to it. Findings reflect the state of the research at the time of posting and may be revised, contradicted, or retracted by later work. Rights to the underlying studies belong to their authors and publishers.

What information this site collects

This site has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking of its own. Three features involve data you choose to provide:

  • Newsletter. If you subscribe to the Weekly Digest, your email address is stored by MailerLite (our email service) and used only to send the newsletter. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing removes you from the list.
  • Reader Queue. Study titles and links you submit, and the votes you cast, are stored in Google Firebase and displayed publicly on this site. Don't include personal information in a submission. Your browser locally remembers which entries you've voted on.
  • Email contact. If you email us, we see your address and whatever you send — used only to respond to you.

Embedded Instagram posts are served by Instagram (Meta), which may set its own cookies and collect data under Meta's privacy policy. This site is hosted on Netlify, which may log standard technical request data (such as IP addresses) to operate the service.

Questions

For questions about any of this — including removing a Reader Queue entry or unsubscribing — email theclinicalperspective@gmail.com.
