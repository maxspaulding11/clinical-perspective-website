About The Clinical Perspective

The Clinical Perspective exists to close the gap between published clinical research and the people it affects. Each post distills a study — its methods, findings, and limitations — into a summary that is rigorous enough for clinicians and clear enough for anyone else.

This site serves as a standing archive of that work: a place to browse past coverage, verify sources against the original posts, and get in touch about new research worth covering.

Submit Your Research

Researchers, clinicians, and authors are welcome to submit published or in-press work for consideration. Priority is given to peer-reviewed studies with clear clinical or public health relevance.

1

Prepare your materials

A link or PDF of the paper (or preprint), plus 2–3 sentences on why it matters clinically.

2

Reach out

Send it by email or Instagram DM using the links below. Include your name, affiliation, and role on the paper, if any.

3

Hear back

Submissions are reviewed on a rolling basis. Not every submission is covered, but every submission is read.
